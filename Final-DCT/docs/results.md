The designs were synthesized targeting a 100 MHz clock frequency.

|----------------|------------------|---------------------------|------------|-------|------|
| D1: Baseline   | 512              | 1/8                       | 105        | 320   | 1    |
| D2: Pipelined  | 512              | 1/8                       | 210        | 450   | 1    |
